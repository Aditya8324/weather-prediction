How to Use the Weather App

1. Searching for Weather Information

To search for weather information for a specific location:

  • Use the search bar at the top of the app interface to enter the name of the city or location.
  • Click on the "Search" button to fetch the weather details for the entered location.
  • The app will display key weather metrics such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, and more.

2. Understanding the Displayed Information

The app provides detailed weather data for the selected location. Here is what each parameter means:

  • Temperature: The current temperature in degrees Celsius.
  • Min/Max Temperature: The lowest and highest temperatures recorded for the day.
  • Feels Like: The perceived temperature based on wind and humidity.
  • Humidity: The amount of moisture in the air, displayed as a percentage.
  • Air Pressure: The atmospheric pressure measured in hPa (hectopascals).
  • Wind Speed: The speed of the wind in kilometers per hour (km/h).
  • Visibility: The distance you can see, measured in meters.
  • Sea Level and Ground Level Pressure: Atmospheric pressure at sea level and the ground level.

3. Using Dropdowns or Options for Different Cities

The app includes a dropdown menu for quick access to weather data for popular cities:

  • Click on the "How to Use?" dropdown in the navigation menu.
  • Select a city from the list (e.g., Delhi, Bangalore, Seattle).
  • The app will fetch and display the weather information for the selected city.

This feature allows users to quickly check the weather without typing the city's name manually.

Additional Tips

  • Ensure you have an active internet connection to fetch real-time weather data.
  • If a city is not listed in the dropdown, use the search feature to find its weather details.
  • Reload the app if you encounter any errors or delays in fetching data.
